How the no-internet part works

1

Pair once. Scan a QR code or tap phones together and confirm a safety emoji. Done — you're connected.

2

Connect directly. On the same Wi-Fi or a hotspot, your phones find each other and talk peer-to-peer — no server in the middle.

3

Stay in sync. Messages and shared plans merge automatically when you reconnect, so nothing is lost offline.
