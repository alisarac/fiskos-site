Private by design — not by promise

Most apps ask you to trust their servers. Fiskos doesn't have any. Privacy isn't a setting here; it's how it's built.

◆No servers, no account. No phone number, no sign-up, no tracking. Your data lives only on your devices.
◆End-to-end encrypted. Only you and the people you pair with can read your messages — no one in the middle, not even us.
◆Encrypted on your phone. Everything is encrypted at rest with a key kept in your phone's secure hardware.
◆App lock. Require a fingerprint, face or PIN to open Fiskos.
◆Disappearing messages. Set any chat to auto-delete after an hour, a day or a week.
◆Panic wipe & verified contacts. Erase everything instantly if you need to, and verify a contact's safety number so no one can impersonate them.

How the no-internet part works

1

Pair once. Scan a QR code and confirm a safety emoji — even fully off-grid over Wi-Fi Direct. Done, you're connected.

2

Connect directly. On the same Wi-Fi, a hotspot, or phone-to-phone, your phones find each other and talk peer-to-peer — no server in the middle.

3

Stay in sync. Messages and shared plans merge automatically when you reconnect, so nothing is lost while you're offline.
